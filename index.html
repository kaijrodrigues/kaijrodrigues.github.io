About

My work sits at the intersection of qualitative and quantitative research: survey design, statistical analysis, interview synthesis, and data visualization, usually in service of a specific decision a team needs to make.

I care about rigor without losing the story — a finding only matters if someone can understand it fast enough to act on it. Most of the analysis and modeling behind these projects is done in R; write-ups and visualizations below link out to the underlying code.
