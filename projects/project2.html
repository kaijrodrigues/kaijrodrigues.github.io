Brand Tracker: Sportsbooks, Event Contracts & Prediction Markets

Brand Tracking Google Trends Power BI 2026

Question

How have consumer interest and brand health shifted across three adjacent but distinct betting categories — legal sportsbooks, event contracts, and prediction markets — as the regulatory landscape evolved and new platforms entered the market? Which category is gaining mindshare, and what events are driving inflection points?

Approach

The tracker combines search-volume data from Google Trends with brand health metrics to build a longitudinal view of category momentum and relative brand standing. Key platforms within each category are tracked head-to-head, with a linked annotation table surfacing the regulatory, product, and media events that correspond to spikes or drops in search interest.

  • Data sources: Google Trends (relative search volume), brand health surveys
  • Categories tracked: Legal sportsbooks (e.g. DraftKings, FanDuel), event contracts (e.g. Kalshi, CFTC-regulated venues), prediction markets (e.g. Polymarket, Manifold)
  • Deliverables: Interactive Power BI dashboard; supporting Excel workbook with raw data and annotation log

Dashboard

The Power BI dashboard is organized into three views: a category-level trends overview, a brand-level comparison within each category, and an event annotation panel linking key dates to observed search-volume and sentiment shifts.

A third panel (below) presents the event annotation table — a log of regulatory decisions, platform launches, and media events that correspond to inflection points in the trend data.
